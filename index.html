About this page

Basically, what you see here is a pixel presentation of 80s he-man action figure series.

I'm something of a collector of these old toys and one day I had an idea to draw a retro pixel art image of one. A small experiment in simplification: how to present a figure in 32x32 pixels. Then I did another, and eventually some more. (If you're interested, I started with the Battle Beasts series, and this MotU catalog is the second one).

Interactivity: Hold mousebutton on a character to reveal character's special gimmick if available.
You can also play with the sort and recolor options: move mouse on a character and click color swatches to recolor single character. At the end of the page there's also an option to recolor all at once.
